Third-Party Notices
===================

This file lists third-party packages used by the ShekelSync desktop app
(app + renderer). It is derived from:
- app/package-lock.json
- renderer/package-lock.json

Full license texts and copyright notices for each dependency are included
with the corresponding package under node_modules in the source tree.
If you distribute a build without node_modules, include those license files
alongside your distribution.
